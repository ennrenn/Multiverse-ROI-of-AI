Recommendations for Leaders

Based on the findings, here are three key actions for leaders to unlock AI maturity

1
Understand your AI maturity position

Have a clear view of your goals, including potential ROI and risks. Get skills intelligence insights to understand your current gaps.

2
Create a future-ready AI skills strategy

Equip teams with skills to leverage AI. Give them tools and clear policies, and measure results across your organization.

3
Create new career opportunities through AI skills

Create access to AI skills to help workers accelerate their career progression. Consider reskilling employees into AI specialist roles.
